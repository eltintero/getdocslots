FAQ

Common questions

What kind of offices is this for?

Any medical or wellness office that rents rooms to independent practitioners — psychologists, therapists, psychiatrists, physical therapists, nutritionists, dermatologists. If you have rooms and providers who use them on a schedule, this is for you.

Can providers book their own appointments?

Yes. Each provider gets their own login and can book, view, and cancel their own appointments. They only see what they need to. Your front desk and admin team see everything.

What happens if two people try to book the same slot?

The system prevents it. Slot availability is checked in real-time — if someone books a slot while you're looking at it, it updates instantly. No double bookings, ever.

Is there a per-seat charge as we add providers?

No. The flat monthly rate covers up to 20 providers. Need more? Talk to us — we'll work something out. But you'll never get a surprise bill because you added a new therapist.

How long does setup take?

Most offices are live within 48 hours. We handle the technical setup — domain, accounts, room configuration, branding. You just tell us what you need.

Can I customize the system for my office?

Absolutely. That's the point. Need custom operating hours? Special cancellation policies? Integration with your billing software? We build it to fit your workflow, not the other way around.

Does it work on phones?

Yes. The entire system is responsive — desktop, tablet, and mobile. Providers can check their schedule and book rooms from their phone.

What if I need help after setup?

Support is included. There's a feedback button built right into the app — click it, describe the issue, and we'll respond. No separate ticketing system to learn.

Is my data secure?

Yes. The system runs on enterprise-grade infrastructure (Convex + Vercel) with encrypted connections, role-based access control, and no shared databases between clients. Your data is yours.
